Overview

What is Arithmancy?

Arithmancy is a powerful mathematical calculator that helps you solve complex mathematical problems with step-by-step explanations. Our tool supports four main mathematical functions:

Quadratic Equations

Solve quadratic equations with visual graphs, find roots, and calculate vertex points.

Fibonacci Sequences

Generate Fibonacci sequences with beautiful spiral visualizations.

Factorial Calculations

Calculate factorials with detailed step-by-step breakdowns.

Prime Number Generation

Generate prime numbers and explore number theory concepts.

How to Use

  1. Navigate to "Solve Now" to access the calculator
  2. Choose from the available mathematical functions
  3. Enter your parameters and click "Solve"
  4. View detailed explanations and visualizations
